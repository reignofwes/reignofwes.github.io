To legally copywrite any website, landing page, or UI you must: Identify copyrightable content Gather the materials you want to copyright. Add a copyright symbol This symbol indicates that the content on your website is yours and cannot be used without your permission. Add a copyright notice This notice at the bottom of your site explicitly tells users that it's copyrighted and identifies you as the copyright holder. While not legally required, it can reduce the likelihood of your work being used without authorization. Complete a copyright registration application Registering your copyright can give you advantages like the ability to sue others for copyright infringement.
